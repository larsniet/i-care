# Privacy Policy

**icare — Eye Care Reminders**

*Last updated: March 25, 2026*

## Overview

icare is designed with your privacy in mind. The app works entirely on your device and does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers.

## Data Storage

All app data — including your reminder settings, break history, and preferences — is stored locally on your iPhone and Apple Watch using on-device storage. This data never leaves your devices.

## Data Sharing

icare does not share any data with third parties. The app contains no analytics, no tracking, no advertising, and no third-party SDKs.

## Device Communication

icare uses Apple's WatchConnectivity framework to sync your settings and reminder state between your iPhone and Apple Watch. This communication happens directly between your paired devices over Apple's secure channel and does not pass through any external server.

## Notifications

icare uses local notifications to deliver break reminders. These are scheduled entirely on your device and do not involve any remote notification service.

## Changes to This Policy

If this policy is updated, the revised version will be posted here with an updated date.

## Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue on the project's GitHub repository.
