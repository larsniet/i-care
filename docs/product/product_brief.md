# Product Brief

## One-Sentence Product

A free, minimal 20-20-20 reminder app for iPhone and Apple Watch that quietly reminds users to look away from their screen and complete a simple 20-second distance-viewing break.

## Problem

People who spend long periods at screens already know they should take visual breaks, but they do not follow the 20-20-20 rule consistently because:

- remembering is easy to forget during focused work
- existing apps are often noisy, bloated, or paid
- phone-only reminders are easy to miss
- many products treat this as a generic wellness problem instead of a tiny utility problem

## Core User Need

"Remind me every 20 minutes, preferably with a subtle tap on my watch, and let me complete a 20-second break with almost no friction."

## Product Promise

Help users follow the 20-20-20 rule without annoying reminders, subscriptions, or clutter.

## Positioning

This product is:

- an iPhone app that is fully useful on its own
- materially better with Apple Watch
- a focused utility, not a wellness platform

This product is not:

- a medical device
- a vision therapy app
- a habit game
- a productivity suite
- a subscription product

## Target Users

Primary users:

- office workers
- developers
- designers
- students
- remote workers
- gamers

High-value segment:

- Apple Watch users who want subtle haptics instead of louder phone-first reminders

## Product Principles

1. The app must be useful without Apple Watch.
2. Apple Watch should feel like the best way to use the product.
3. The default experience should be quiet and non-intrusive.
4. Scope must stay tiny and focused.
5. The core loop must feel frictionless.

## Core Loop

1. Reminder fires.
2. User taps once.
3. Countdown starts.
4. Break completes.
5. Next reminder is scheduled automatically.

## Default Behavior

- reminder interval: 20 minutes
- break duration: 20 seconds
- haptics: on
- sound: off
- schedule: active during user-defined working hours
- weekday filter: optional

## Success Definition

The MVP succeeds if:

- setup takes under one minute
- reminders feel subtle rather than annoying
- the countdown is fast to start
- watch users clearly prefer it over phone-only alternatives
- iPhone-only users still get enough value to keep it installed
